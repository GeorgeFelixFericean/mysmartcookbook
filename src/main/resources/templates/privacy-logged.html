Privacy Policy

At MySmartCookbook, your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services, including your name, email address, and recipes you choose to save.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To store and display your recipes
  • To send optional account-related emails (activation, reset, contact)

3. Data Storage

Your data is securely stored on servers within the European Union. We do not share your data with third parties.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to ensure the proper functioning of our website and to enhance your experience. Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies – These are required for the core functionality of the website, such as managing your login session. They cannot be disabled.
  • No tracking or advertising cookies – We do not use cookies for marketing, tracking, or third-party advertising.

By continuing to browse the site, you agree to our use of essential cookies. You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time. You can contact us using the contact form or by email at contact@mysmartcookbook.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy in the future. We'll notify users through the app if important changes are made.

If you have any questions about this policy or your data, feel free to reach out via the contact form or by emailing us at contact@mysmartcookbook.com.

This policy is provided for informational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice.
