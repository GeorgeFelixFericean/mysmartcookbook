Why I Created MySmartCookbook

I never knew what to cook. Most days, I’d open the fridge, find a few ingredients, and wonder what I could do with them.

I searched online, but recipe websites weren’t helpful — you can’t search by what you already have. And worse, all my favorite recipes were scattered: handwritten notes, bookmarks, YouTube saves, Facebook posts… chaos.

I didn’t come up with any of these recipes — but they’re mine in the sense that I’ve cooked them and enjoyed them. Now they all live in one clean, personal space — often with a link to the original source.

Most recipe platforms push their own content. But this isn’t “yet another recipe site.” MySmartCookbook gives you control: save your own recipes, organize them how you like, and search them by name or ingredients you already have.

What makes it different?
  • Save your personal recipes – with notes, links, and ingredients
  • Search by what’s in your fridge – partial matches included
  • It’s your cookbook – not someone else’s ad-filled blog
What I believe in:
  • Your data stays yours – nothing is sold or tracked
  • No ads, no distractions – just your kitchen
  • This is just the beginning – I’m constantly improving it

Thank you for trusting MySmartCookbook. It’s a small project with a big heart.
